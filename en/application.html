The call will be open from April 18 to May 23, 2022. To apply please refer to this online form, where among other requisites, the following materials are requested:
Resume or short bio (1 page)
Statement of interest (500 words)
5–10 work samples
Work samples should be recent and can include images and texts as well as links to websites, videos, and/or audio-based works.

Any questions regarding the application process may be sent to info@materiaabierta.com, with the subject “Application 2022.”
